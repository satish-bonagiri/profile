About Me

I’m Satish Bonagiri — a Senior Android Engineer, Technical Leader, and M.Tech graduate from IIT Kharagpur with 13+ years of experience building and scaling mobile products across FinTech, Retail, Healthcare, Education, Classifieds, and Wearables.

Throughout my career, I have transformed ideas into production-ready applications for leading organizations including PayPal, Tesco, Samsung, and Quikr. My work spans the complete product lifecycle — from architecture and technical strategy to implementation, optimization, release, and long-term scalability.

I specialize in Android architecture, Kotlin, Java, Jetpack Compose, Wear OS, and modern mobile engineering practices, with a strong focus on performance, usability, maintainability, and business impact.

Beyond engineering, I mentor teams, establish technical standards, conduct design reviews, and collaborate closely with Product, UX, and Analytics stakeholders. I also leverage AI-powered workflows using Gemini, Claude, and ChatGPT to accelerate delivery, improve code quality, and enhance engineering productivity.
